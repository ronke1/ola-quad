Investor Registration

If looking to invest, everyone knows buildings provides best form of investments over years. Our investments cycle is 2 to 3 years for returns, which means within 36 months you can have your capital plus interests back in your bank coffers.

How It Works

You can register your investment interest anytime during the year, using our application form. Please ensure all areas are completed properly.

  1. Your funds will go into our Joint Venture Partner account, overseen by a Barrister of law.
  2. You will be issued with a company investment receipt / certificate with your promisory rate upon completion of the registration process.
  3. You would also be added to our database of investors, enabling you to log into our investor page for updates on how your investment is doing, and any information for investors
