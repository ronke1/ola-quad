Construction Department

Here we manage actual site buildings, from receiving working brief, through receipt of drawings to final completion of client homes. Lots of time onsite coordinating of contractors and taking responsibility for Health, Safety and Environment to International standards.

Commercial Department

We take responsibility in this department for procurements, customer needs, cost and contract management, all legal requirements, and ensures collaboration with all other teams and stakeholders, to ensure ontime delivery within agreed budgets. Ensures we are focused on client demography.

Manufacturing Department

We focus on managing the design, development and production of modules and panels using best environmentally friendly materials. Here you will be working closely with factory staff or in the factory as trades, and collaborate with commercial & construction departments to ensure smooth transition from design through to delivery of housing.>
