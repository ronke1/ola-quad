About Us

Who We Are

OLA-QUAD is a modern development company, though we have over 20 years UK experience.

Our technical expertise combined with use of modern construction methods is put towards creating exceptional sustainable and affordable homes for you. We have an expert in-house design team that creates perfect homes to international building codes, structurally sound and engineered to deliver best functional requirements.

Only the highest quality of craftsmanship goes into creating our buildings, our customer service is excellent and our wealth of expertise is evident in every project.
We take pride in our products, and would be delighted to show you around our factory & show homes, so you can gain a feel for our attention to detail which sets us apart from competitors.

Our Vision

To be a world class business, defined by exceptional use of sustainable materials to create impeccable living spaces.

Our strategy is People, Places, Communities, Environment & Operations. Skills, Knowledge and dedication alongside efficient management are fundamental to delivery of this vision.
