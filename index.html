BAMBOO FACTS

Climate Change

  • Bamboo grows to maturity within 5years, compared to Wood/Trees taking 100 years plus.
  • Bamboo consumes 5 times more Carbon Monoxide than any other plant.
  • Bamboo stops soil erosion and land degradation.

Energy Consumption

  • Bamboo consumes 87.5 % less Energy in processing than Wood.
  • Bamboo consumes 98% less Energy in processing than Steel.

Economic Sustainability

  • Bamboo Timber is Stronger than mild Steel.
  • Bamboo is used in over 4000 commercial products including Airplanes.
