Tiny Homes

What is a Tiny House?

Some call this a movement because it is becoming popular in North America. Tiny is now a common adjective for some would consider ‘small’ or miniscule living space, it is an economical living space which is now fashionable due to having less impact on the environment

It is now the pinnacle of conscientious living, with style & character, built to high international Building Codes, support heating, cooling & energy conservation & thereby reduces environmental Impact for benefits of current & future generations

Why Tiny?

Current technological advances such as slim Tv, mobile phones have made going tiny living more possible, Tiny living is not a new idea.
